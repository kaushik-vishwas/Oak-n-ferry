Who We Are?

Welcome to Oak n Ferry!

Located in the vibrant heart of Woolston Village, Oak n Ferry is more than just a sports bar; it's a community hub where locals gather to unwind, indulge, and create lasting memories.

  • At Oak n Ferry, we take pride in our strong connection to the Woolston community. With a warm and inviting atmosphere, we welcome guests from all walks of life to enjoy our offerings.

Our venue boasts a prime location at 608 Ferry Road in Christchurch, New Zealand, easily accessible for both locals and visitors alike. Whether you're dropping by for a quick bite, catching up with friends over drinks, or cheering on your favorite sports team, Oak n Ferry is your go-to destination.

We believe in providing an exceptional experience for our patrons, which is why we offer a diverse menu featuring delicious dishes that are sure to satisfy your cravings. From hearty pub classics to lighter fare, there's something for everyone to enjoy. Plus, with our outdoor seating area, you can soak up the sunshine while relishing your meal or drinks.

  • Indulge in the best cocktails and food in Christchurch, expertly crafted by our talented team of mixologists and chefs. Our innovative cocktail menu features an array of signature drinks that will tantalize your taste buds and keep you coming back for more.

But Oak n Ferry is not just about great food and drinks; it's about fostering a sense of community and belonging. We regularly host events and promotions, such as free pool days and special meal deals, to bring people together and create memorable moments.
